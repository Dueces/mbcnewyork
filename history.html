Our History

Myanmar Baptist Church, New York was established in 1995. We thank God for watching over us through all these years, and for all those whom God had put in our lives to help establish this church. We are greatful for our senior pastor, Rev. U Myo Maw who has been with us since the early days of our church.

We urge all the members of the church to be one in Christ, to dedicate more of our gifts and blessings to His service, so that MBCNY be a living and thriving church, a church that is favored by God and has a good standing among people, a church that gives glory to God and to His Name.
